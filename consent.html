Preregistration Challenge

Notice of Consent

We made this Challenge to encourage the practice of pre-registration while being as fair and transparent as possible. Below are eight items that we need to make sure you understand before you begin. We welcome questions (prereg@cos.io).

  1. Research plans that you submit through this process will be reviewed by Pre-Reg administrators and reviewers.
  2. You must also submit your published article for review before receiving the prize, and the article must meet these guidelines.
  3. Prizes will be awarded at pre-determined dates to eligible entrants. If more eligible entrants exist than available prizes, entrants will be ranked based on date of registration.
  4. Articles can only be published in these eligible journals.
  5. The use of pre-existing data may prohibit you from entering the Pre-Reg Challenge.
  6. Prizes are given to the person who submits the research plan, who must be listed as an author on the published article.
  7. Residents of countries on this list cannot receive a prize.
  8. You must read and agree to the complete terms and conditions.
