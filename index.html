Welcome to the Preregistration Challenge!

The process of pre-registering your plans is beneficial to both the scientific field and to you, the scientist. By writing out detailed data collection methods, analysis plans, and rules for excluding or missing data, you can make important decisions that affect your workflow earlier, without the biases that occur once the data are in front of you.Learn more about the Preregistration Challenge.

Ready for the Challenge?

  1. Specify all your study and analysis decisions prior to investigating your data
  2. Publish your study in an eligible journal
  3. Receive $1,000
